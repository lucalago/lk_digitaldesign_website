► COMPENSATION MODELS

I believe there is no one-size-fits-all. In this sense, I am also prepared and willing to work based on different compensation models.

Retainer fee or Recurring fee

The classic model includes the payment of a fee in exchange for the services. I will also take cryptocurrencies.

Shared revenue or performance based

A model where I participate in the results of the project through a determined percentage or based on performance.

Equity or Stock option

A differentiated model where I receive a share (or have the option to receive a share) of the business/project as a payment for my work.
